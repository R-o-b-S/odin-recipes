Carciofi alla Tapi

This is Tapi's secret recipe for artichokes. "Who's Tapi?" you ask.
Well, that's a secret just like this recipe. Sharing it I'm putting my own safety on the line.

Let's start with ingredients:

  • artichokes
  • white wine
  • parsley
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • pepper
  • salt

Now that you got all the necessary let's cook!

  1. clean your artichokes. We only need it's delicious and soft heart
  2. cut artichokes hearts in half
  3. finely cut parsley
  4. spill some olive oil in your pan, add one clove of garlic and turn on heating
  5. when oil is ready add artichokes and salt them directly in the pan
  6. stir and then apply the pan lid for 5 minutes
  7. blend with wine
  8. add parsley and pepper, then stir
  9. pour little water and leave it cooking 10 more minutes. This time no lid
  10. enjoy

Optional: use ground chili pepper instead of pepper

Now that the recipe is done and you know Tapi's secret I go into hiding fearing the day an hitsquad made of cooks will come for me.
