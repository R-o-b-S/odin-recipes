Carbonara

Carbonara, a name that echoes through every dining all in Italian resturants.
The story of this simple and tastefull dish is heavely debated so we are not going to delve in it.
What matters the most to us now is the only important thing. It is delicious.

At least following the original recipe.

Many degenerates contaminate this sacred dish using nefarious ingredients like Panna (cooking cream).
yhea, I'm talking to you northen Italians.
In cooking is nice to experiment new things and try variations but if you really want to touch pasta Nirvana stick with me this time.

The following recipe is the traditional one. Lets start with ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Guanciale (better) or Bacon
  • Pecorino cheese
  • Pepper
  • coarse salt

Now that you have all the necessary lets cook!

  1. Fill a pot with water and put it to boil
  2. While water is boiling:
    1. Cut guanciale in little pieces
    2. Break eggs separating yolk, we only need that
    3. Mix yolk with a lot of Pecorino cheese and pepper. If using a differnt cheese then pecorino add salt too
  3. When water is boiling add coarse salt and spaghetti
  4. Quickly put guanciale in a pan at high temperature for 2 minutes and then leave it fry at low temperature
  5. when spaghetti is al dente take them out of the pot and put them in the pan with guanciale
  6. Add little cooking water from spaghetti to the pan and stir all togheter until there is almost no water left
  7. Turn off the heating and quickly add yolk mixed beforehand
  8. Gently stir all togheter
  9. Put little extra Pecorino and Pepper on top
  10. Enjoy
